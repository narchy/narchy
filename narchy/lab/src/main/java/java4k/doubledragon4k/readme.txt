Double Dragon 4k
================

Description
-----------

Battle through 6 levels of intense fighting to rescue Billy's girlfriend, Marian, from the Black Warriors, 
the savage street gang of the mysterious Shadow Boss!

The legend of the shadow.... "If the illusion spreads, the evil will live again. 
But, if two dragons soar through the sky, an angel will fall to the Earth."

Instructions
------------

Use the arrow keys or the WASD keys to move. Press any other key to attack.
